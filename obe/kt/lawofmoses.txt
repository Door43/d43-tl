====== Batas ng Diyos, Kautusan, Kautusan ng Diyos, Batas ni Moses ======

===== Kahulugan: =====

Ang salitang “Ang Kautusan” o “Kautusan ni Moses” ay tumutukoy sa mga batas na ibinigay ng Diyos kay Moses para sundin ng mga Israelita. Maaari din itong tawaging “Batas ng Diyos” o “Kautusan ng Diyos.”

  * Tinutukoy din nito ang “Sampung utos” na ibinigay ng Diyos kay Moses sa bundok Sinai.
  * Ang unang limang Akalt ng Lumang Tipan sa Bibliya.
  * Tinutukoy din nito ang buong Lumang Tipan sa Bibliya.

===== Mga Mungkahi sa Pagsasalin: =====

  *
  * Since there are many instructions, some translations may prefer to translate this in the plural and say, "laws."
  * The "law of Moses" could be translated as "the laws that God told Moses to give to the Israelites."
  * Depending on the context, "the law of Moses" can also be translated as "the law that Moses wrote" or "God's laws that Moses wrote down" or "the laws that God told Moses to give to the Israelites."
  * Ways to translate "the law" or "law of God" or "God's laws" could include: "laws from God" or "God's commands" or "laws that God gave" or "everything that God commanded" or "God's rules" or "the laws that God commanded."
  * The phrase, "law of Yahweh" could also be translated as, "Yahweh's laws" or "laws that Yahweh said to obey" or "laws from Yahweh" or "things Yahweh commanded." (Also see translation suggestions above for "law of God" and "God's laws".)

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** God also gave many other **laws**  and rules to follow. If the people obeyed these **laws**, God promised that he would bless and protect them. If they disobeyed them, God would punish them.\\

  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:15-13|[15-13]]]** Then Joshua reminded the people of their obligation to obey the covenant that God had made with the Israelites at Sinai. The people promised to remain faithful to God and follow **his laws**.\\

  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:21-05|[21-05]]]** In the New Covenant, God would write **his law**  on the people's hearts, the people would know God personally, they would be his people, and God would forgive their sins.\\
