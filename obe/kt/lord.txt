====== panginoon ======

===== Kahulugan: =====

Ang salitang “Panginoon” ay ginagamit para tukuyin ang isang tao na nagmamay-ari sa isang bahay o lupain. Maaari din itong gamitin para tukuyin si Jesus, o ang mga taong may alipin.

  * Panginoon ang tawag ng mga alipin sa nagmamay-ari sa kanila noong unang panahon.
  * Ang “Panginoon” ay ginagamit din para tukuyin ang Diyos, “Panginoong Diyos” o “Panginoong Jesus.” Ito ay nangangahulugang ang Diyos ang may kapangyarihang mamahala at mamuno sa lahat ng tao at sa lahat ng nilikha.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term should be translated as "master" when it refers to a person who owns slaves. It could also be used by a servant to address the person he works for.
  * When it refers to Jesus, it could be translated as "master" if the context shows it means "religious teacher."
  * If the person addressing Jesus does not know him, "lord" could be translated as "sir." This translation would also be used for other contexts in which a polite form of address to a man is called for.
  * When referring to God the Father or to Jesus, this term is written as "Lord" (capitalized). Make sure the translation clearly distinguishes between "lord" that just means "master" and "Lord" that refers to God.
