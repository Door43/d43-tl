====== maniwala, maniwala sa, paniniwala ======

===== Kahulugan: =====

Magkalapit ang ibig sabihin ng mga salitang "maniwala" at "maniwala sa" ngunit may konting pagkakaiba:

=== 1. maniwala ===

  * Ang "maniwala" ay pagtanggap o pagsang-ayon sa isang bagay na ito ay isang katotohanan.

  * Kapag naniniwala tayo sa isang tao, umaaayon tayo na totoo ang sinasabi niya.

=== 2. maniwala sa ===

  * Kung naniniwala tayo sa isang tao, ang ibig sabihin ay pinagkakatiwalaan natin siya. Naniniwala tayo sa kung sino siya ayon sa kanya. Naniniwala tayo na ang taong ito ay palaging nagsasabi ng totoo at tutuparin niya kung ano ang mga pangako niya
  * When a person truly believes in something, he will act in such a way that shows that belief.
  * The phrase "have faith in" usually has the same meaning as "believe in."
  * To "believe in Jesus" means to believe that he is the Son of God, that he is God himself who also became human and who died as a sacrifice to pay for our sins. It means to trust him as Savior and live in a way that honors him.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * To "believe" could be translated as to "know to be true" or "know to be right."
  * To "believe in" could be translated as, "trust completely" or "rely on and know to be true."
