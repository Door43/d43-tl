====== Rebekah ======


===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Si Rebekah ay anak ng isa sa mga pamangkin ni Abraham. Kapatid ni Abraham ang lolo niya.

  * Si Rebekah ang pinili ng Diyos na maging asawa ng anak ni Abraham na si Isaac.
  * Iniwan ni Rebekah ang bansa niya at sumama sa lingkod ni Abraham papunta sa bansa kung saan naroon si Isaac, pinakasalan niya si Isaac at tumira siya doon kasama ng pamilya niya.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:06-02| [06-02]]]** After a very long journey to the land where Abraham's relatives lived, God led the servant to **Rebekah**. She was the granddaughter of Abraham's brother.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:06-06| [06-06]]]** God told **Rebekah**, "There are two nations inside of you."
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:07-01| [07-01]]]** As the boys grew up, **Rebekah** loved Jacob, but Isaac loved Esau.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:07-03| [07-03]]]** Isaac wanted to give his blessing to Esau. But before he did, **Rebekah** and Jacob tricked him by having Jacob pretend to be Esau.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:07-06| [07-06]]]** But **Rebekah** heard of Esau's plan. So she sent Jacob far away to live with her relatives.
