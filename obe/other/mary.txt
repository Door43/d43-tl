====== Mary ======


===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Si Mary ang asawa ni Joseph.

  * Siya rin ang ina ni Jesus. Sa pamamagitan ng kapangyarihan ng Banal na Espiritu, mahimala niyang ipinagbuntis ang Anak ng Diyos na si Jesus kahit birhen pa siya.
  * Naging asawa ni Joseph si Mary ngunit nanatili siyang birhen hanggang sa naipanganak si Jesus.
  * Nang ipinanganak ni Mary ang sanggol, Jesus ang ipinangalan nila sa kanya.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:22-04|[22-04]]]** When Elizabeth was six months pregnant, the same angel appeared to Elizabeth's relative, whose name was **Mary**. She was a virgin and was engaged to be married to a man named Joseph. The angel said, "You will become pregnant and give birth to a son. You are to name him Jesus and he will be the Messiah."
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:22-05|[22-05]]]** The angel explained, "The Holy Spirit will come to you, and the power of God will overshadow you. So the baby will be holy, the Son of God." **Mary** believed and accepted what the angel said.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:22-06|[22-06]]]** Soon after the angel spoke to **Mary**, she went and visited Elizabeth. As soon as Elizabeth heard **Mary's** greeting, Elizabeth's baby jumped inside her.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:23-02|[23-02]]]** The angel said, "Joseph, do not be afraid to take **Mary** as your wife. The baby in her body is from the Holy Spirit."
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:23-04|[23-04]]]** Joseph and **Mary** had to make a long journey from where they lived in Nazareth to Bethlehem because their ancestor was David whose hometown was Bethlehem.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:49-01|[49-01]]]** An angel told a virgin named **Mary** that she would give birth to God's Son. So while she was still a virgin, she gave birth to a son and named him Jesus.
