====== Uriah ======


===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Si Uriah ay isang matuwid na tao at isa siya sa mga pinakamagaling na sundalo ng haring si David. Si Uriah ay may magandang asawa na si Bathsheba.

  * Nagkasala si David dahil sa pangangalunya niya kay Bathsheba. Nabuntis si Bathsheba at nagkaroon sila ng anak ni David.
  * Upang mapagtakpan ang nagawa niyang kasalanan gumawa ng paraan si David para mamatay si Uriah sa digmaan pagkatapos ay naging asawa niya si Bathsheba.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:17-12|[17-12]]]** Bathsheba's husband, a man named **Uriah**, was one of David's best soldiers. David called **Uriah** back from the battle and told him to go be with his wife. But **Uriah** refused to go home while the rest of the soldiers were in battle. So David sent **Uriah** back to the battle and told the general to place him where the enemy was strongest so that he would be killed.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:17-13|[17-13]]]** After **Uriah** was killed, David married Bathsheba.
