====== Caleb ======

===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Si Caleb ay isa sa labindalawang mga Espiya na inutusan ni Moses na magmanman sa Canaan.

  * Si Joshua kasama si Caleb ay Hinimok ang mga Israelita na magtiwala sa Diyos na masasakop nila ang Canaan kahit pa higante ang mga tagaroon.

(Mungkahi sa Pagsalin: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])

=====   =====

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:14-04|[14-04]]]** When the Israelites reached the edge of Canaan, Moses chose twelve men, one from each tribe of Israel. He gave the men instructions to go and spy on the land to see what it was like. 
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:14-06|[14-06]]]** Immediately **Caleb** and Joshua, the other two spies, said, "It is true that the people of Canaan are tall and strong, but we can certainly defeat them! God will fight for us!"
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:14-08|[14-08]]]** "Except for Joshua and **Caleb**, everyone who is twenty years old or older will die there and never enter the Promised Land."
