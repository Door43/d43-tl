====== Amorite ======


===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

ang mga Amorite ay isang lahi ng mga tao na naninirahan sa canaan noong panahon ni Joshua.

  * Naninirahan ang mga Amorite malapit sa ilog Jordan. Bawat lungsod ay pinamumunuan ng hari.
  * Ipinangako ng Diyos na ibibigay niya ang Canaan sa mga Israelita, pati na rin ang lupain ngn mga Amorites.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:15-07|[15-07]]]** Sometime later, the kings of another people group in Canaan, the **Amorites**, heard that the Gibeonites had made a peace treaty with the Israelites, so they combined their armies into one large army and attacked Gibeon. 
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:15-08|[15-08]]]** In the early morning they surprised the **Amorite** armies and attacked them. 
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:15-09|[15-09]]]** God fought for Israel that day. He caused the **Amorites** to be confused and he sent large hailstones that killed many of the **Amorites**.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:15-10|[15-10]]]** God also caused the sun to stay in one place in the sky so that Israel would have enough time to completely defeat the **Amorites**.
