====== Jacob ======

===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Si Jacob ay ang mas bata sa kambal na anak ni Isaac at Rebekah. Si Esau ang mas naunang kakambal.

  * Ang pangalan ni Jacob ay nangangahulugan ng "manloloko."
  * Matalino at magaling manloko si Jacob. Niloko niya si Esau at ang ama nilang si Isaac nang agawin niya ang pagpapala para sa panganay na para sana kay Esau, kaya galit nag alit si Esau sa kanya pero dumating din ang panahon na pinatawad niya si Jacob at namuhay sila ng tahimik sa Canaan.
  * Pinalitan ng Diyos ang pangalan ni Jacob ng “Israel” na ang ibig sabihin ay “Nakipaglaban siya sa Diyos.”
  * May labindalawang anak na lalaki si Jacob at sila ang pinagmulan ng labindalawang lahi ng Israel.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:07-01|[07-01]]]** As the boys grew up, Rebekah loved **Jacob**, but Isaac loved Esau. **Jacob** loved to stay at home, but Esau loved to hunt.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:07-07|[07-07]]]**  **Jacob** lived there for many years, and during that time he married and had twelve sons and a daughter. God made him very wealthy.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:07-08|[07-08]]]** After twenty years away from his home in Canaan, **Jacob** returned there with his family, his servants, and all his herds of animals.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:07-10|[07-10]]]** The covenant promises God had promised to Abraham and then to Isaac now passed on to **Jacob**.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:08-01|[08-01]]]** Many years later, when **Jacob** was an old man, he sent his favorite son, Joseph, to check on his brothers who were taking care of the herds.
