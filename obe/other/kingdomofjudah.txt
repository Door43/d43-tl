====== Judah, kaharian ng Judah ======

===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Ang Judah ang pinakamalaki sa labindalawang tribo ng Israel. Pagkamatay ni haring Solomon, nahati sa dalawa ang kaharian ng Israel: ang hilagang Israel at ang Judah. Ang kaharian ng Judah ay ang timog na kaharian, binubuo ito ng tribo ng Benjamin at Judah.

  * Ang kabisera ng kaharian ng Judah ay Jerusalem.
  * Ang ilan sa mga naging hari sa kaharian ng Judah ay sumunod sa Diyos at pinangunahan ang mga tao sa pagsamba sa Kanya, ngunit ang karamihan sa mga naging hari ay masasama.
  * Noong panahon ng paghahari ni Nebuchadnezzar, sinalakay ng mga kawal niya ang kaharian ng Judah at dinala nila ang karamihan sa mga mamamayan nito sa Babylon at nanatili sila doon sa loob ng 70 na taon.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:18-07|[18-07]]]** Only two tribes remained faithful to him (Rehoboam). These two tribes became the **kingdom of Judah**.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:18-10|[18-10]]]** The **kingdoms of Judah** and Israel became enemies and often fought against each other.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:18-13|[18-13]]]** The **kings of Judah** were descendants of David. Some of these kings were good men who ruled justly and worshiped God. But most of **Judah's** kings were evil, corrupt, and they worshiped idols.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-01|[20-01]]]** The **kingdoms of Israel and Judah** both sinned against God.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-05|[20-05]]]** The people in the **kingdom of Judah** saw how God had punished the people of the kingdom of Israel for not believing and obeying him. But they still worshiped idols, including the gods of the Canaanites.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-06|[20-06]]]** About 100 years after the Assyrians destroyed the kingdom of Israel, God sent Nebuchadnezzar, king of the Babylonians, to attack the **kingdom of Judah**.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]** Nebuchadnezzar and his army took almost all of the people of **the kingdom of Judah** to Babylon, leaving only the poorest people behind to plant the fields.
