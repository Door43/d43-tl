====== Jerusalem ======

===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Ang Jerusalem ang kabisera ng Israel.

  * Ang templo ay sa Jerusalem ipinatayo ni haring Solomon na anak ni aring David.
  * Sa Jerusalem din nilitis at hinatulang mamatay si Jesus sa pamamagitan ng pagpako sa kanya sa krus.
  * Ilang beses din nagpakita si Jesus sa mga alagad niya pagkatapos niyang mabuhay at bago siya bumalik sa langit.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:17-05|[17-05]]]** David conquered **Jerusalem**  and made it his capital city.
  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:18-02|[18-02]]]** In **Jerusalem**, Solomon built the Temple for which his father David had planned and gathered materials.
  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-07|[20-07]]]** They (Babylonians) captured the city of **Jerusalem**, destroyed the Temple, and took away all the treasures of the city and the Temple.
  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-12|[20-12]]]** So, after seventy years in exile, a small group of Jews returned to the city of **Jerusalem**  in Judah.
  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:38-01|[38-01]]]** About three years after Jesus first began preaching and teaching publicly, Jesus told his disciples that he wanted to celebrate this Passover with them in **Jerusalem**, and that he would be killed there.
  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:38-02|[38-02]]]** After Jesus and the disciples arrived in **Jerusalem**, Judas went to the Jewish leaders and offered to betray Jesus to them in exchange for money.
  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:42-08|[42-08]]]** "It was also written in the scriptures that my disciples will proclaim that everyone should repent in order to receive forgiveness for their sins. They will do this starting in **Jerusalem**, and then go to all people groups everywhere."
  * **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:42-11|[42-11]]]** Forty days after Jesus rose from the dead, he told his disciples, "Stay in **Jerusalem**  until you receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you."
