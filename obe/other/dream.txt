====== panaginip ======

===== Kahulugan: =====

Ang panaginip ay ang mga bagay na nikikita, naririnig at nararanasan ng mga tao habang sila ay tulog.

  * Katulad ng kay Joseph. (O.T.) nangusap ang Diyos sa panaginip niya.
  * Kadalasan, ang mga napapanaginipan ng mga tao ay hindi nagkakatotoo pero sa panaginip ay mukha itong totoo.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:08-02|[08-02]]]** Joseph's brothers hated him because their father loved him most and because Joseph had **dreamed** that he would be their ruler.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:08-06|[08-06]]]** One night, the Pharaoh, which is what the Egyptians called their kings, had two **dreams** that disturbed him greatly. None of his advisors could tell him the meaning of the **dreams**.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:08-07|[08-07]]]** God had given Joseph the ability to interpret **dreams**, so Pharaoh had Joseph brought to him from the prison. Joseph interpreted the **dreams** for him and said, "God is going to send seven years of plentiful harvests followed by seven years of famine."
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:16-11|[16-11]]]** So that night, Gideon went down to the camp and heard a Midianite soldier telling his friend about something he had **dreamed**. The man's friend said, "This **dream** means that Gideon's army will defeat the Midianite army!"
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:23-01|[23-01]]]** He (Joseph) did not want to shame her (Mary), so he planned to quietly divorce her. Before he could do that, an angel came and spoke to him in a **dream**.
