====== Abraham, Abram ======


===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Si Abraham ang taong pinili ng Diyos para pagmulan ng lahi ng mga Israelita.

  * Nangako ang Diyos kay Abraham na magkakaroon siya ng maraming apo at sila ay pagmumulan ng makapangyarihang bansa.
  * Sumunod at nanalig si Abraham sa Diyos.
  * "Abram"  ang dating pangalan niya pero pinalitan ito ng Diyos ng "Abraham."
  * Ang ibig sabihin ng Abraham ay “Ama ng marami.”

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:04-06|[04-06]]]** When **Abram** arrived in Canaan, God said, "Look all around you. I will give to you and your descendants all the land that you can see as an inheritance."
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:05-04|[05-04]]]** Then God changed **Abram**'s name to **Abraham**, which means "father of many."
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:05-05|[05-05]]]** About a year later, when **Abraham** was 100 years old and Sarah was 90, Sarah gave birth to Abraham's son.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:05-06|[05-06]]]** When Isaac was a young man, God tested **Abraham's** faith by saying, "Take Isaac, your only son, and kill him as a sacrifice to me."
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:06-01|[06-01]]]** When **Abraham** was very old and his son, Isaac, had grown to be a man, **Abraham** sent one of his servants back to the land where his relatives lived to find a wife for his son, Isaac.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:06-04| [06-04]]]** After a long time, **Abraham** died and all of the promises that God had made to him in the covenant were passed on to Isaac.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:21-02|[21-02]]]** God promised **Abraham** that through him all people groups of the world would receive a blessing.
