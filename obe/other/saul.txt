====== Saul (OT) ======


===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Si Saul ay isang Israelita na pinili ng Diyos para maging unang hari ng Israel.

  * Si Saul ay matangkad, magandang lalaki at magaling na sundalo. Siya ang uri ng tao na gusto ng mga Israelita na maging hari nila.
  * Noong una ay sinusunod ni Saul ang Diyos pero hindi nagtagal, naging mayabang siya at sinuway niya ang Diyos, dahil dito pinili ng Diyos si David upang pumalit kay Saul bilang hari.

Daang taon ang lumipas, may isa pang Hudyo ang dumating na nagngangalan ding Saul, pero ang kaniyang panngalan ay pinalitan din bilang Paul matapos niyang makilala si Jesus.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:17-01|[17-01]]]** **Saul** was the first king of Israel. He was tall and handsome, just like the people wanted. **Saul** was a good king for the first few years that he ruled over Israel.   ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:17-04|[17-04]]]** **Saul** became jealous of the people's love for David. **Saul** tried many times to kill him, so David hid from **Saul**. 
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:17-05|[17-05]]]** Eventually, **Saul** died in battle, and David became king of Israel.
