====== Saul (O.T.) ======

===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Si Saul ay isang Israelita na pinili ng Diyos para maging unang hari ng Israel.

  * Si Saul ay matangkad, gwapo at magaling na sundalo. Siya ang klase ng tao na gusto ng mga Israelita na maging hari nila.
  * Noong una ay sinusunod ni Saul ang Diyos pero hindi nagtagal, naging mayabang siya at sinuway niya ang Diyos, dahil dito pinili ng Diyos si David upang pumalit kay Saul bilang hari.
  * Lilipas ng daan-daang taon, may isa pang Hudyo ang darating, "Saul" din ang pangalan niya, pero  di-magtatagal makikilala niya si Jesus at papalitan ang pangalan niya ng "Paul."

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:17-01|[17-01]]]** **Saul** was the first king of Israel. He was tall and handsome, just like the people wanted. **Saul** was a good king for the first few years that he ruled over Israel. But then he became a wicked man who did not obey God, so God chose a different man who would one day be king in his place.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:17-04|[17-04]]]** **Saul** became jealous of the people's love for David. **Saul** tried many times to kill him, so David hid from **Saul**. 
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:17-05|[17-05]]]** Eventually, **Saul** died in battle, and David became king of Israel.
