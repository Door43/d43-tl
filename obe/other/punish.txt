====== parusa, kaparusahan ======

===== Kahulugan: =====

Ang ibig sabihin ng “parusa” o pagpaparusa ay pagpapahirap sa isang tao dahil sa nagawang kasalanan.

  * Pinarusahan ng Diyos ang mga Israelita dahil sa pagsuway nila sa kanya at higit pa doon sumamba sila sa mga diyus-diyosan.
  * Kadalasan ang kaparusahan ay para sa mga taong tinuturuan upang hindi na muling gawin ang pagkakasala  kagaya ng ginawa ng Diyos nang parusahan niya ang mga Israelita.
  * Pinarusahan si Jesus para sa kasalanang nagawa ng bawat isa.Tinanggap niya ang kaparusahan na nagawa ng bawat isa kahit wala siyang nagawang kasalanan at kahit hindi karapat-dapat sa kanya ang kaparusahan.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:13-07|[13-07]]]** God also gave many other laws and rules to follow. If the people obeyed these laws, God promised that he would bless and protect them. If they disobeyed them, God would **punish** them. 
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:16-02|[16-02]]]** Because the Israelites kept disobeying God, he **punished** them by allowing their enemies to defeat them. 
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:19-16|[19-16]]]** The prophets warned the people that if they did not stop doing evil and start obeying God, then God would judge them as guilty, and he would **punish** them. 
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:48-06|[48-06]]]** Jesus was the perfect high priest because he took the **punishment** for every sin that anyone has ever committed.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:48-10|[48-10]]]** When anyone believes in Jesus, the blood of Jesus takes away that person's sin, and God's **punishment** passes over him.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:49-09|[49-09]]]** But God loved everyone in the world so much that he gave his only Son so that whoever believes in Jesus will not be **punished** for his sins, but will live with God forever.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:49-11|[49-11]]]** Jesus never sinned, but he chose to be **punished** and die as the perfect sacrifice to take away your sins and the sins of every person in the world.
