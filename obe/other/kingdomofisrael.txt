====== kaharian ng Israel ======

===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Nang mamatay si Haring Solomon, nahati sa dalawa ang Kaharian ng Israel. Isa rito ay ang Hilagang kaharian, binubuo ito ng sampung tribo ng Israel.

  * Lahat ng mga naging hari sa hilagang Israel ay pawang masasama dahil hinimok nila ang mga tao na huwag nang sambahin ang Diyos sa templo sa may Jerusalem, sa halip ay maglingkod na lang at sumamba sa mga diyos-diyosan. Kaya inutusan ng Diyos ang mga taga - Assyria na salakayin sila, ipatapon sa Assyria at gawing bihag.
  * Maraming mga taga-Assyria ang nanirahan sa Israel nang masakop nila ito. Nakipag-asawa sila sa mga Israelita roon at tinawag na Samaritano ang mga anak at kaapu-apuhan nila.
  * Samaria ang kabisera ng kaharian ng Hilagang Israel.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:18-08|[18-08]]]** The other ten tribes of the nation of Israel that rebelled against Rehoboam appointed a man named Jeroboam to be their king. They set up their kingdom in the northern part of the land and were called the **kingdom of Israel**.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:18-10|[18-10]]]** The **kingdoms of Judah and Israel** became enemies and often fought against each other.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:18-11|[18-11]]]** In the new **kingdom of Israel**, all the kings were evil.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-01|[20-01]]]** The **kingdoms of Israel** and Judah both sinned against God.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-02|[20-02]]]** The **kingdom of Israel** was destroyed by the Assyrian Empire, a powerful, cruel nation. The Assyrians killed many people in the **kingdom of Israel**, took away everything of value, and burned much of the country.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-04|[20-04]]]** Then the Assyrians brought foreigners to live in the land where the **kingdom of Israel** had been. The foreigners rebuilt the destroyed cities and married the Israelites who were left there. The descendants of the Israelites who married foreigners were called Samaritans.
