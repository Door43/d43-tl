====== Pulang Dagat ======

===== Mga Katotohanan: =====

Ito ay anyo ng tubig na naghihiwalay sa Hilagang Africa at Arabian Peninsula.

  * Ang Pulang Dagat ay mahaba at makitid. Mas malaki ito sa isang lawa o ilog, pero mas maliit sa isang karagatan.
  * Kinakailangang tawirin ng mga Israelita ang Pulang Dagat noong tumatakas sila mula sa Egypt.

(Tignan rin sa: [[:tl:obe:other:egypt|Egypt, Egipcio]], [[:tl:obe:kt:promisedland|Lupang Pangako]])


===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:12-04|[12-04]]]**  When the Israelites saw the Egyptian army coming, they realized they were trapped between Pharaoh's army and the **Red Sea**.
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:12-05|[12-05]]]**  Then God told Moses, "Tell the people to move toward the **Red Sea**."
  ***[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:13-01|[13-01]]]**  After God led the Israelites through the **Red Sea**, he led them through the wilderness to a mountain called Sinai.
