====== Psalms Chapter 102 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What do the afflicted pray for Yahweh not to do in their time of trouble?

A. The afflicted pray for Yahweh not to hide his face from them in their time of trouble. [102:2]

Q? What does the afflicted compare his crushed heart to?

A. The afflicted says that he is like grass that has whithered. [102:4]

Q? What has happened due to the afflicted's continual groaning?

A. With the afflicted's continual groaning, he has became very thin. [102:5]
