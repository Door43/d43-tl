===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues to remind the adults to teach their children God's mighty deeds.

  * **Dathan and Abiram, the sons of Eliab** - Moses is referring to an event in the past when Dathan and Abiram rebelled against Moses and Aaron. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **earth opened its mouth and swallowed them up** - This means the land split open and the people fell in. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **But your eyes have seen** - AT: "But you have seen" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
