===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Moses sang in the ears of all the assembly of Israel** - AT: "Moses sang this song to all the assembly of Israel" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **all the assembly of Israel** - This refers to the Levites and to the elders and officers from all the tribes.
  * **the words of this song**  - "the words of the song that Yahweh taught him"
