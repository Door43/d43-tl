===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **joyfulness and gladness of heart** - These mean the same thing. They emphasize that the people should have been very glad to worship Yahweh. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **​He will put a yoke of iron on your neck** ​- This means Yahweh will allow the enemy to treat the Israelites cruelly and make them slaves. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
