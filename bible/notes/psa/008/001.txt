===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:tl:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]].

  * **set to the gittith style** - Possible meanings: 1) "This song is sung to a tune of the people of Gath." 2) "This song is sung in the style of the people of Gath."
  * **A psalm of David** - AT: “This is a psalm that David wrote.”  
  * **Yahweh our Lord** - AT: "Yahweh, you who are our Lord," or "Yahweh, you who rule over us," 
  * **how magnificent is your name in all the earth** - God's "name" represents his whole being. AT: "people all over the world know that you are very great!" (UDB) (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Out of the mouths of babies and infants you have created praise** - AT: "You have given babies and infants the ability to praise you"
