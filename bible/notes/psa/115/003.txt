===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Our God is in heaven** - AT: "Our God has supreme power" or "Our God has the utmost power." (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Our God**  - The word "Our" refers to the people of Israel.
  * **whatever he pleases** - "whatever he wants"
  * **The nations' idols are silver and gold** - "The idols of the nations are made of silver and gold"
  * **the work of men’s hands** - AT: "The result of men's effort." (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
