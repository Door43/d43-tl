===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Oh, send out your light ... let them lead me **- The words your light and your truth have the same meaning. AT: "Please give me your truths to guide my life" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **holy hill**  - The Temple in Jerusalem is referred to by the hill it is built on. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **exceeding joy**  - AT: "very great joy"
