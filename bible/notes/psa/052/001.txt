===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **mighty man**  - This refers to Doeg the man from Edom who made trouble with King Saul for David
  * **razor**  - A sharp blade cutting off hair on the face.
  * **Your tongue…like a sharp razor**  - Here the tongue is compared to a sharp razor which is capable of causing great harm (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
