===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues to speak to the lord.
 
  * **on the day of your power ** - or "when you gather your army"
  * **from the womb of the dawn** - Yahweh describes the dew as a baby to whom the dawn gives birth. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **from the womb of the dawn your youth will be to you like the dew.**- AT: "Your youthful strength will act for you like the dew that waters the earth early in the morning."
