===== Translation Notes: =====

This psalm is a song of praise.\\ 
See: [[tl:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]


  * **from that foreign people** - "from a people that served a different god." (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Judah** - "The people of Judah"
  * **became his sanctuary** - "became the people with whom Yahweh dwelled" or "became holy to Yahweh" 
  * **Israel his kingdom** - AT: "Israel became the people over whom Yahweh ruled."
