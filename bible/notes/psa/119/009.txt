===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **BETH**  - This is the second letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 9-16 begins with this letter.
  * **How can a young person keep his path pure?** - This question is used to introduce a new value to the word of God. AT: "This is how a young person can keep his path pure" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **I seek you**  - Here this means "I try to serve you."
  * **stray from your commandments**  - AT: “disobey your commandments.” (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
