===== Translation Notes: ==== 
  * **gave the enemy into the hand of Israel** - Here the word "hand" represents control. Also, "Israel" is a shortened name for the nation of Israel. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **attacked them with the sword** - attacked them with weapons (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Misrephothmaim**  - this is the name of a place. (See: [[tl:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
