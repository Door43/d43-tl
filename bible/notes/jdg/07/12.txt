===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **as thick as a cloud of locusts**  - The army camp is compared to a swarm of locusts because there are many people camped in a small area. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **they were more in number than the grains of the sand on the seashore **  - AT: "There were a great number of camels." (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
