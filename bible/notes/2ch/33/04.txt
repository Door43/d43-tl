===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my name will be placed **- This means only Yahweh will be worshipped. His name is inscribed in Jerusalem as a mark of ownership. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **for all the stars of heaven** - AT: "for false gods"
  * **Valley of Ben Hinnom** - A place near Jerusalem also known as Gehenna. 
  * **he performed soothsaying and sorcery, read omens, and consulted with those who talked with the dead, and with those who talked with spirits** - AT: "he did many things prohibited in the Law of Moses"
