===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **leave us or forsake us** - The two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **incline our hearts to him** - AT: "make us devoted to him" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **live in all his ways** - AT: "live as he requires us to live" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
