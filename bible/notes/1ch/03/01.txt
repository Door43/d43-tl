===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ahinoam...Abigail...Maacah...Haggith...Abital...Eglah**  - These are the names of women. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Talmai...Shephatiah...Ithream** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Daniel** - This person has the same name as the Israelite prophet but is a different person.
