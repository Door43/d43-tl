===== Translation Notes: =====

A new poem begins. See [[tl:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] â€‹and [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]].

  * **Call to mind** - AT: "Remember" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Look and see our shame** - "Look at the shameful state we are in"
  * **Our inheritance has been turned over to strangers** - AT: "You have allowed strangers to take possession of our inheritance" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **our houses to foreigners** - "you have allowed foreigners to take possession of our houses" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **We have become orphans ... our mothers are like widows** - The people of Jerusalem have no one to protect them because the men have either died in battle or have gone into exile. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Our water costs silver to drink** - "We have to pay silver to our enemies in order to drink our own water"
  * **our own wood is sold to us** - AT: "our enemies sell us our own wood" (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
