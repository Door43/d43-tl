===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh's house**  - "the Lord's temple"
  * **Yahweh of hosts**  - AT: "Yahweh who rules over all"
  * **to this city and to all of its towns**  - AT: "to the people of this city and the nearby towns" (See: Metonymy)
  * **they stiffened their neck**  - This phrase means "they were stubborn" because animals that stubbornly refused to accept the yoke were said to be stiff-necked. (See: Explicit and Implicit)
