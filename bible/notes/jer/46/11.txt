===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh finishes his declaration to Egypt.

  * **virgin daughter of Egypt**  - AT: "Unspoiled people of Egypt". (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **disgrace - **  This word means a condition of feeling ashamed or the loss of respect.
  * **The earth is filled with your laments​**  - AT: everyone can hear your crying. (See: [[:tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
