===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to tell Isaiah what he should tell Ahaz. (See: [[tl:bible:notes:isa:07:03]])
  * **Aram, Ephraim, and the son of Remaliah** - The words "Aram" and "Ephraim" refer to the kings of these lands. Also, "Ephraim" refers to the northern kingdom of Israel. AT: "Rezin the king of Aram and Pekah the son of Remaliah, king of Israel. (See: [[tl:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Remaliah** - See how you translated this in [[tl:bible:notes:isa:07:01|7:1]]
  * **have planned evil against you**  - Here "you" is singular and refers to Ahaz. (See: [[tl:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **son of Tabeel**  - It is unknown who this man is. (See: [[tl:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
