===== How to Translate in translationStudio =====

This module answers the question: How can I translate in translationStudio?\\
Before you start this module have you learned about:
  * [[tl:ta:vol1:process:pretranslation_training|Training Before Translation Begins]]
  * [[tl:ta:vol1:process:setup_tsandroid|Setting Up translationStudio]]

==== Using Translation Helps ====

  - Once the source text is [[tl:ta:vol1:process:setup_tsandroid|setup]] on translationStudio, then you are ready to start translating. 
    * It is strongly recommended that you consult the translation notes and key terms while doing your translation
  - For the best way to use translation notes in translationStudio, please read [[tl:ta:vol1:tech:ts_resources]]

==== Editing a page ====

  * To learn how to create or edit a page click [[tl:ta:vol1:tech:ts_create|here]]
  * If your language has letters that do not appear on your device's keyboard, you may need to install and use [[tl:ta:vol1:tech:tk_intro|translationKeyboard]]. (See also https://unfoldingword.org/translationkeyboard/ )

==== Saving My Work ====

  * To learn how to save your work click [[tl:ta:vol1:tech:ts_create|here]]

==== Sharing Your Work ====

  * To learn how to share your work click [[tl:ta:vol1:tech:ts_share|here]]

==== After Translating on translationStudio ====

  - We strongly recommend that your translation be [[tl:ta:vol1:process:prechecking_training|checked]].
  - Once the checking is complete (to any level), you may [[tl:ta:vol1:tech:ts_upload|upload]] your work.
  - Once uploaded, you will need to [[tl:ta:vol1:process:upload_merge|merge]] your work into Door43.
