====== 21. Ipinangako ng Diyos ang Pagdating ng Messiah ======

===== Pangkalahatang Kaisipan: =====

God promised to send the Messiah to defeat Satan and to bless every people group. The prophets said the Messiah would be a prophet like Moses, a king like David, and the great high priest of the New Covenant. He would be born from a virgin, perform miracles, and be rejected and killed by the people. But the Messiah would rise from the dead and rule forever.
