===== Gumaling si Naaman [19-15] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-15.jpg?nolink&}}

Nang marinig ito ni Naaman nagalit siya at ayaw niya itong gawin dahil mukhang hindi ito kapani-paniwala. Pero hindi nagtagal naniwala rin siya at inilublob niya ang sarili niya ng pitong beses sa Ilog Jordan. Sa ikapitong pag-ahon ni Naaman nawala ang nakakadiring sakit niya sa balat dahil pinagaling siya ng Diyos!

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **would not do it because it seemed foolish** - Naaman would not do what Elisha said because he knew that washing alone could not heal his disease.
  * **he changed his mind** - That is, "he decided to do what Elisha had told him to do."
