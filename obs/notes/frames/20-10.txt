===== Inalala ng Diyos ang Pangako Niya [20-10] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-10.jpg?nolink&}}

Kahit pinarusahan ng Diyos ang mga tao dahil sa kasalanan nila sa pamamagitan ng pagpapatapon, hindi sila kinalimutan ng Diyos pati ang mga pangako niya sa kanila. Patuloy pa rin silang ginagabayan at kinakausap ng Diyos sa pamamagitan ng mga propeta. Ipinangako niya na ibalik sila sa Lupang Pangako pagkatapos ng 70 na taon.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **exile** - See how you translated this term in **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]**.
  * **did not forget** - This could be translated as, "did not ignore" or, "did not neglect." Or the whole phrase could be translated as, "continued to honor his commitment to his people and his promises."
  * **watch over** - That is, "take care of."
  * **speak to them through his prophets** - This could be translated as, "tell his prophets messages that he wanted them to tell his people."
