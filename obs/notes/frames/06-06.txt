===== Ang Pagmumulan ng Dalawang Bansa [06-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Sinabi ng Diyos kay Rebekah, “Dalawang bansa ang mabubuo mula sa mga anak mong kambal na lalaki. Lalabanan nila ang isa’t-isa at pagsisilbihan ng panganay ang mas nakakabata.”

===== Mga Mahahalagang Salita: =====

  * **[[:tl:obe:kt:god|Diyos]]**
  * **[[:tl:obe:other:rebekah|Rebekah]]**
  * **[[:tl:obe:kt:son|anak na lalaki]]**

===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **from the two sons** - That is, "from the descendants of the two sons."
  * **They will struggle with each other** - Both the two sons and the nations that will come from them will continually fight with each other. Compare to **[[:tl:obs:notes:frames:06-05|[06-05]]]**.
  * **the older son** - Even though the babies were twins, the baby that came out first would be treated as the older son.
  * **serve the younger one** – This could be translated as, "the older son will have to do what the younger one tells him to do."
