===== Mensahe ng Diyos Para Kay Zechariah [22-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Sa mga lumipas na panahon ang Diyos ay nakikipagusap sa mga tao sa pamamagitan ng mga anghel at propeta, ngunit paglipas ng 400 na taon, hindi sila kinausap ng Diyos. Pero biglang may isang anghel mula sa Diyos ang dumating na mensahe para kay Zecariah, isang matandang pari. Si Zecariah at asawa niyang si Elizabeth ay makadiyos pero hindi pinalad na magkaanak si Elizabeth.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his people** - This could be translated as, "his people, the Israelites" or "his people, the Jews." But only include the added information if it is not clearly understood who these people were.
  * **400 years went by** - Another way to say this would be, "400 years passed" or "there were 400 years." 400 years had passed since the last Old Testament prophet, Malachi.
  * **when he did not speak to them** - That is, "during which God did not give any messages to the prophets for his people."
  * **godly people** - That is, "people who obeyed God."
