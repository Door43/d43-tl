===== Ang Pagdilim [10-11] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Pinagdilim rin ng Diyos ang Egypt sa loob ng tatlong araw. Hindi makaalis sa kani-kanilang mga bahay ang mga Egipcio sa sobrang dilim pero maliwanag naman kung saan nakatira ang mga Israelita.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **God sent darkness** - God caused darkness to cover or spread over most of Egypt. In other words, God took away the light from this part of Egypt.
  * **darkness that lasted for three days** - This darkness was darker than ordinary nighttime darkness, and it continued to be totally dark for three whole days.
